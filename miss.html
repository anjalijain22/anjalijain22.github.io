I miss you too :(

Just thinking about it fills me up with so much happiness. I know you probably miss my stupid face but trust me we'll meet soon and oh god the fun we'll have. Its going to be LIT AF!

Oh oh and also remember going up to the 9th floor hostel stairs to have long deep difficult conversations. Ah such good times! If I actually sit to write down each and every one of them then damn I am going to be in tears! So why don't you just call me for now and let talk about the stupid random tatti shit we do and laugh our ass off!

Lots of love,
